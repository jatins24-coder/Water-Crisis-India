Shocking Data Points

  • 70% of India's surface water is unfit for consumption.
  • 21 cities will run out of groundwater by 2030, affecting 100 million people.
  • 600 million Indians face high to extreme water stress.
  • Only 18% of rural households have access to piped water.
  • 75% of households do not have drinking water on their premises.
